e-BuonWeekend

Our Mission

When we started this journey, our product owner, Rodica, told us that after a week of work, she wanted just to relax during the weekend and in order to do that, she asked us to create something that allow her to exchange her house with someone else's, for just a weekend, without any payment or fee, with just an handshake.

That's how e-BuonWeekend was born.


How It Works

e-BuonWeekend is a platform that allow you to exchange your house with someone else's, for just a weekend and without any kind of fees or payments. Our platform connects people to unique travel experiences, in more than 65.000 cities and 191 countries. And with world-class customer service and a growing community of users, e-BuonWeekend is the easiest and cheapest way for people to travel wherever they want.

You need just few steps to enjoy your e-BuonWeekend experience:
- Register and add your house
- Search for your favourite location
- Send an HAND to someone else's house and wait for his answer
- Get in touch with that user and complete the exchange process with just an.. HANDSHAKE!


The Team
